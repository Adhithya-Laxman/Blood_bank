Types of Blood Donation

Discover how donating blood can save lives and contribute to the well-being of others,
while also benefiting your own health through improved circulation and the potential for reduced risk of certain health conditions

Whole Blood

The most common type where a pint of blood is collected, including red cells, plasma, and platelets.

Plasma Donation

Only the plasma component is collected, which is used in the treatment of burn victims, patients with clotting disorders, and certain autoimmune diseases.

Platelet Donation

Platelets are collected through a process called apheresis and used in the treatment of cancer patients, organ transplant recipients, and individuals with blood disorders.
